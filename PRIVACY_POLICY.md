# WaitSmart Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 2026**

## Who we are

WaitSmart is an independent NHS patient navigation app 
built by Dilshad Ahmad, Stoke-on-Trent, England.

WaitSmart is not affiliated with or endorsed by NHS England 
or the National Health Service.

## What data we collect

WaitSmart collects the following information that you 
voluntarily provide during onboarding:

- Your first name
- Your NHS specialty (e.g. Cardiology, Dermatology)
- Your hospital name
- Your referral date

## Where your data is stored

**All data is stored exclusively on your device.**

We do not operate any servers. We do not collect, transmit, 
or store your personal data on any external systems. 
Your information never leaves your phone.

## What data we do NOT collect

- We do not collect your NHS number
- We do not collect your date of birth
- We do not collect your email address
- We do not collect your location
- We do not collect any medical records
- We do not use any analytics or tracking tools
- We do not use cookies

## NHS waiting time data

WaitSmart displays publicly available NHS England 
Referral to Treatment (RTT) waiting times statistics.

Source: NHS England RTT Waiting Times Statistics
URL: https://www.england.nhs.uk/statistics/statistical-work-areas/rtt-waiting-times/

This data is open government data published under the 
Open Government Licence v3.0.

## Medical disclaimer

WaitSmart provides information only. It is not a medical 
device and does not provide medical advice. Always consult 
your GP or healthcare provider before making any decisions 
about your care.

## Your rights

Since we store no data on our servers, there is no personal 
data for us to delete, export, or modify on your behalf. 
You can delete all app data at any time by:

1. Opening WaitSmart
2. Going to Profile
3. Tapping "Reset app and start over"

Or by uninstalling the app from your device.

## Changes to this policy

We will update this policy if our data practices change. 
The latest version will always be available at:
https://github.com/dilshadblp/WaitSmart
